About Shuyu Zhang

Shuyu Zhang is a multidisciplinary artist and designer working at the intersection of technology, nature, and traditional art forms. Her practice explores the poetic relationships between organic systems and digital interfaces.

Born in Shanghai and educated at Central Saint Martins (BA) and the Royal College of Art (MA), Zhang's work has been exhibited internationally, including at the Venice Biennale's collateral events, Ars Electronica, and the Shanghai Biennale.

Her recent research focuses on:

  • Biomimetic interfaces that respond to environmental data
  • Algorithmic reinterpretations of traditional ink painting
  • Sound sculptures using organic materials as conductors

Zhang is currently based between London and Beijing, where she leads the experimental design studio Aperture Fields.
